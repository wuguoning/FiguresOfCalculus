## Surfaces of $z = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}$ and $z^2 = x$

The upper surface is $z^2 = x$, and the lower surface 
is $z = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$. The projection of the volume
on xy plane is $(x-1)^2 + y^2 \le 1$.
